The product

Quiet tools for confident hosts.

Porch sits in the background of how you already host. A light dashboard to track your stays, a clean Welcome for your guests, and a Record that assembles itself when you need it most.

03. The Record
When you need it, it's already written.

If a stay goes sideways — a retaliatory review, a chargeback, a dispute — the Record is ready. Porch assembles the evidence, drafts the letter in Airbnb's own policy language, and hands you a one-click submission. The tool does the hard work; you review, edit, send.

Dispute request · Reservation HMQ8EACB4B
Re: Retaliatory review by Nancy S.

Dear Airbnb Case Manager,

I am writing to request removal of the review left by Nancy S. for reservation HMQ8EACB4B under Airbnb's Retaliatory Review Policy.

On October 14, 2025 at 2:47 PM, the guest signed a written acknowledgment of the property's house rules, including prohibitions on smoking, unregistered guests, and quiet hours. The signed agreement is attached.

During the stay, the guest violated multiple rules: a neighbor complaint was received on October 16 at 10:47 PM regarding noise, and physical evidence of smoking was documented on October 17 at 9:12 AM with photographs. These violations were reported to the guest and to Airbnb through the Resolution Center on October 18.

The guest's 1-star review was posted on October 19 at 3:14 PM — approximately 27 hours after I submitted the reimbursement claim for smoking-related deep cleaning. The timing and content of the review establish a clear retaliatory pattern consistent with the policy's three-part test: documented violation, guest notification, and biased review following enforcement.

This is my first dispute request in six years of hosting with a 4.98 average rating across 142 reviews. I would be grateful for your review of the attached evidence.

Respectfully,
Ricky N.

Every sentence of the dispute letter is drafted from the evidence Porch already has — nothing to fabricate, nothing to remember. The Record writes itself because the Welcome filed the groundwork months earlier.

What your guests see — calm, clear, 90 seconds.
